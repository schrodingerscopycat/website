Introduction

Hello world, welcome to Schrödinger's Copycat. A new site destined to teach you the beauty of this world.

This webpage is dedicated to those crazy ones that want to understand maths, technology, artificial-intelligence through the beauty of its core.

The name of this site "Schrödinger's Copycat is in honor of a black and white kitten named "Mabel". She was as close as a sister or a daughter to me, always by my side. In my incompetence of taking care of her, one day she disappeared. Thinking of the worst and still hoping she's alive.

Schrödinger's Copycat Nothing in this world is completly original, eveyone is a copy of someone else to a greater or lesser extent because of our environment, influences, learnings, customs, ideologies and so on. We always are a part of someone else with a combination of our personality, giving as result a new identity, "one-self". Be proud of yourself!
